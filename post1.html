How I Survived Final Year Nursing School in Nigeria

Final year in nursing school is not for the weak.

There were days I was broke. Days I was stressed. Days I wanted to give up.

Clinical postings, exams, assignments, night duties — it felt endless.

But I learned something powerful:

1. Discipline is everything

You must study even when you don’t feel like it.

2. Clinical confidence matters

Ask questions. Practice procedures. Don’t be shy.

3. Protect your mental health

Rest when you can. Pray. Stay around positive people.

4. Remember your WHY

I chose nursing because I wanted to save lives and build a better future.

If you are in nursing school right now, don’t give up. You are stronger than you think.

- Paul Sunday
